From the Heart to Cleanliness 💙
The story of our company is not just a business tale — it’s a real journey of strength, faith, and care.
It all started in the summer of 2022, when Maria — a refugee from Ukraine — arrived in Portugal with
nothing but a desire to survive and be useful. No connections, no language, no financial safety net…
just a big heart and a willingness to work. She took the first cleaning jobs herself — with a mop and
broom in hand. We cleaned everything: dusty apartments, old houses, post-rental messes, and
even abandoned properties untouched for years.
But everything changed. Soon, like-minded people joined Maria. From just one order per week, we
grew to dozens every day. Today, our team includes 12 full-time professionals and more than 15
seasonal workers. We clean not only apartments and villas, but also restaurants, offices, and
commercial properties. Yes — even a yacht!
We have thousands of satisfied customers all over the world. After all, a happy and satisfied customer is the most important thing for us. Our goal is to make all our customers happy and satisfied.
